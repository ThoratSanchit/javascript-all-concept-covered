Q)Difference between for..of and for..in?
   for..of:--   1) Usage: Loop through iterable objects (arrays, strings, etc.)
                2)Returns Values: Returns values of the iterable.

   for..in--- 1) Usage: Loop through the enumerable properties of an object.
               2) Returns Keys: Returns keys (property names) of the object.
